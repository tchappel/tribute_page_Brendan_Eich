The man who created JavaScript
Brendan Eich

    Here's a time line of Brendan Eich's life:

  • 1961 - Born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, U.S.
  • 1983 - Eich received his bachelor's degree in mathematics and computer science at Santa Clara University.
  • 1985 - He received his master's degree in 1985 from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
  • 1985 - Eich started his career at Silicon Graphics, working on operating system and network code.
  • 1995 - He started work at Netscape Communications Corporation, and created the basic version of Javascript, in only ten days.
  • 1998 - Eich co-founded the Mozilla project with Jamie Zawinski and others, creating the mozilla.org website.
  • 2005 - He became CTO of the newly founded Mozilla Corporation.
  • 2014 - He was promoted to CEO of Mozilla Corporation.
  • 2015 - Eich became CEO of Brave Software, an Internet security company.
